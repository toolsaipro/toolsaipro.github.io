❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this ID card merger tool free?
Yes, completely free. No account, no subscription, no hidden fees. All features including HD download are available to everyone at no cost.
Are my ID documents safe? Will they be uploaded to a server?
Your documents are 100% safe. The entire merging process happens locally in your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images are never uploaded to any server, never stored, and never transmitted over the internet. When you close the page, all image data is cleared automatically from your browser memory.
What image formats are supported?
You can upload JPG, JPEG, PNG, WebP and most other common image formats. The merged output can be downloaded in JPEG, PNG or WebP format.
What if my front and back images have different sizes?
No problem. The tool automatically handles different image sizes. For horizontal merge, the canvas height is set to the taller of the two images, and both images are centered vertically. For vertical merge, the canvas width is set to the wider image with centering applied. No distortion, no cropping.
Can I merge documents other than Aadhar and PAN cards?
Absolutely. This is a general-purpose ID card and document merger. It works for any two images — driving licence, voter ID, passport, employee ID, student card, insurance documents or any other two-sided document.
