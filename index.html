About ToolsAI Pro

ToolsAI Pro is a free online tools platform built to make everyday digital tasks simple, fast and accessible to everyone. Whether you need to merge PDF documents, compress images for your website, generate secure passwords, create QR codes for your business, calculate GST for invoices, check your BMI or convert between units — we have built a dedicated tool for every need.

Every tool on ToolsAI Pro runs entirely within your web browser. This means your files, data and personal information never leave your device. We never upload your files to any server, we never store your data and we never share anything with third parties. Our commitment to privacy is absolute and unconditional.

ToolsAI Pro is continuously growing. We regularly add new tools based on what users need most. All tools are tested on both desktop and mobile to ensure they work perfectly on every device. If you have a suggestion for a new tool, we would love to hear from you via our Contact page.
