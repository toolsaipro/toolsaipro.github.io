About ToolsAI Pro

ToolsAI Pro is a free online tools platform designed to make everyday digital tasks simple and accessible for everyone. Whether you need to merge PDF files, compress images, convert file formats, generate secure passwords, create QR codes, calculate GST or count words — we have a tool for every need.

We believe that powerful digital tools should be available to everyone without barriers — no accounts, no payments and no complicated software to install.
