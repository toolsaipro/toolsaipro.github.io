⚙️ 3. How Our Tools Work (Technical)

All 12 tools on ToolsAI Pro use client-side JavaScript processing. This means:

  • When you upload a file, it is read by your browser's FileReader API — locally, on your device.
  • Processing (merging, compressing, converting) happens using the HTML5 Canvas API and JavaScript — locally, in your browser's memory.
  • Results are generated as downloadable Blob URLs — locally, never passing through any server.
  • The Web Crypto API is used for the Ultra-Secure Password Generator — generating true cryptographic randomness locally.
  • When you close the browser tab or refresh the page, all data is automatically cleared from memory.

You can verify this independently by opening any of our tools, then disconnecting your device from the internet — every tool will continue to work perfectly, proving that no network communication is required or used during operation.

🍪 5. Cookies & Local Storage

ToolsAI Pro itself does not set any tracking cookies or analytics cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any similar tracking technology on our own behalf.

Some of our tools use your browser's sessionStorage or localStorage to temporarily hold state — for example, the password history shown in the Ultra-Secure Password Generator, or the conversion history in the All-in-One Unit Converter Pro Online. This data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's storage in your browser settings.

Google AdSense, as described in Section 4, may set its own advertising cookies. These are governed by Google's cookie policy, not ours.
