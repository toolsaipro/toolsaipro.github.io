🔒 Your Privacy is Fully Protected — Client-Side Processing

When you convert a PDF using most online tools, your file is uploaded to a remote server, processed on that server, and made available for download — often with your file retained for hours or even days after you close the browser. For most documents this may seem harmless, but for sensitive PDFs — bank statements, legal documents, medical reports, ID scans or confidential business reports — this represents a serious privacy risk.

How Our Browser-Based Conversion Works

ToolsAI Pro's PDF to JPG converter works entirely differently. When you upload a PDF, the file is loaded directly into your browser's local memory using the JavaScript FileReader API. Mozilla's pdf.js library — the same open-source engine that powers Firefox's built-in PDF viewer — renders each page directly onto an HTML canvas element in your browser tab. The canvas is then converted to a JPG image using the browser's native toDataURL API. The entire pipeline runs on your own device, using your own processor, within your own browser.

There is no network request during this process. No API call. No file upload. No server receives your data. The converted images exist only in your browser's memory and are downloaded directly to your device. When you close the tab, all data is automatically cleared from your browser's memory by the operating system.

🔒 Privacy Guarantee: ToolsAI Pro has zero server-side components for this tool. We have no technical ability to access, view, intercept or store any PDF or image you process here. Your documents are processed entirely on your own device.
