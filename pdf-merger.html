📄 About PDF Merger Tool

The ToolsAI Pro PDF Merger is a free, browser-based tool that allows you to combine two or more PDF files into a single, unified PDF document. Whether you are a student submitting multiple assignment files, a professional compiling reports and invoices, a job seeker combining a resume with certificates, or anyone who needs to manage PDF documents efficiently — this tool is designed for you.

Unlike desktop software such as Adobe Acrobat which requires a paid subscription, our PDF Merger works entirely within your web browser at absolutely no cost. There is no need to install any application, create an account or provide any personal information. Simply open the tool, upload your files, click merge and download your combined PDF within seconds.

Our tool is built using the PDF-Lib JavaScript library, which enables high-quality PDF manipulation directly in the browser. This means your files never leave your device and are never transmitted to any external server — ensuring complete privacy and security for your sensitive documents.

📋 How to Use — Step by Step

1

Click Browse PDF Files

Click the upload area or the Browse PDF Files button to open the file picker on your device.

2

Select Your PDF Files

Choose 2 or more PDF files from your device. You can select multiple files at once by holding Ctrl (Windows) or Command (Mac) while clicking, or simply tap multiple files on mobile.

3

Review the File List

All selected files appear in a list with their names and sizes. Click the X button next to any file to remove it from the list if needed.

4

Click Merge Now

Click the Merge Now button to begin combining your PDF files. A progress bar will show the merging status. The process takes only a few seconds.

5

Download Your Merged PDF

Once complete, click Download Merged PDF to save the combined file to your device. The file is immediately ready to use, share or print.

🔒 Safety and Privacy

Your privacy and document security are our top priorities. Here is exactly how ToolsAI Pro handles your PDF files:

✅ Your files never leave your device. All PDF processing is performed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript. No data is transmitted to our servers at any point during or after the merge process.

When you click Merge Now, the PDF-Lib library reads your selected files directly from your device memory, combines them into a single document within your browser, and provides the merged file as a downloadable object — all without any network communication.

Once you close or refresh the page, all file data is automatically cleared from your browser memory. We store no user data, no file names and no document content of any kind. This makes ToolsAI Pro one of the safest online PDF tools available.

  • No server uploads — Files are never sent outside your device
  • No data logging — We do not record any information about the files you process
  • No account required — No personal information is collected
  • Automatic data clearing — All file data is erased when you leave the page

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PDF Merger really free to use?
Yes, completely free. There are no hidden fees, no subscription required and no premium plan to unlock features. Every feature of the PDF Merger is available to all users at no cost, and this will never change.
How many PDF files can I merge at once?
There is no limit on the number of PDF files you can merge in one operation. You can combine 2 files, 10 files or even more in a single merge. The only practical limitation is your device's available memory for very large files.
Can I merge password-protected or encrypted PDF files?
Yes. Our PDF Merger is built with the ignoreEncryption option enabled, which means it can process and merge password-protected or encrypted PDF files without requiring you to enter the password. This makes it compatible with a wide range of real-world documents.
Will the merged PDF maintain the original quality?
Yes. The PDF Merger copies all pages from your input files directly into the merged document without any compression, conversion or quality reduction. Fonts, images, layout and formatting are all preserved exactly as in the original files.
Does the tool work on mobile phones?
Yes. The PDF Merger is fully optimised for mobile devices and works on Android phones, iPhones and tablets. Open toolsaipro.github.io in your mobile browser, tap the PDF Merger tool and follow the same steps as on a computer.
Is there a file size limit for each PDF?
There is no strict file size limit imposed by the tool. Since processing happens in your browser, very large files may take slightly longer to process depending on your device performance. For best results, we recommend files under 100MB each.
