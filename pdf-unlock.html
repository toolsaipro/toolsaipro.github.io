❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF unlocker safe to use?
Absolutely. Because ToolsAI Pro uses 100% client-side processing, your PDF files are never uploaded to any server. The unlocking process — including the password you enter — happens entirely within your browser's local memory. No data is transmitted or stored anywhere.
Can I unlock my Aadhaar Card or bank statement PDF?
Standard bank statements and government forms unlock instantly. However, Indian Aadhaar Cards and some newer bank documents use military-grade AES-256 encryption. Because we refuse to upload your documents to a remote server, our browser-based tool cannot decrypt AES-256 files. We prioritise your data privacy above all else.
Is this tool completely free?
Yes — 100% free with no restrictions. No daily limits, no file size caps, no signup required, no watermarks added to the unlocked PDF. ToolsAI Pro is committed to keeping all tools permanently free for everyone.
Can I use this on a mobile phone?
Yes. The tool is fully mobile-responsive and works on Android and iOS in Chrome, Safari and Firefox. You can upload a PDF from your phone's storage or Google Drive, enter the password, and download the unlocked file directly to your device.
What if I forgot the PDF password?
If the PDF requires a password to open (User Password) and you have forgotten it, this tool cannot help — it does not crack or guess passwords. You would need to obtain the password from the document's creator. However, if your PDF only has owner/printing restrictions without an open password, the tool may remove those without any password at all.
How fast is the unlocking process?
Most PDFs unlock in 1–3 seconds because all processing happens locally on your device — there is no server round-trip. The speed depends only on your device's CPU and the PDF's size, not your internet speed. Very large PDFs (50MB+) may take 5–15 seconds on older devices.
