❓ Frequently Asked Contact Questions

How long does it take to get a reply?
We aim to reply to all messages within 24 hours on business days (Monday to Saturday). During busy periods, it may take up to 48 hours. We read and respond to every message personally.
Can I suggest a new tool for the website?
Absolutely! We love hearing suggestions from our users. Select "Tool Suggestion" in the subject dropdown and describe what tool you would find useful. Many of our existing tools were inspired by user feedback.
My file didn't process correctly. What should I do?
Please contact us with as much detail as possible: which tool you were using, what type of file you uploaded, which browser and device you are on, and what error or unexpected result occurred. Screenshots are very helpful. We will investigate and fix any bugs as quickly as possible.
Are ToolsAI Pro tools really free? What's the catch?
Yes, completely free — no catch. We sustain the website through Google AdSense advertisements displayed on some pages. The ads fund server hosting costs and allow us to keep all tools free for everyone. We never charge users, never impose file size limits and never require account registration.
How do I report a privacy or security concern?
Please email us directly at karnaray.tools@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Concern" or "Security Issue". We take all such reports very seriously and will investigate and respond within 24 hours for any security-related matters.
